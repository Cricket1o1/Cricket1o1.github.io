History

The ranch was homesteaded in 1902 by Bill Sweeny in the Big Sheep Basin near Dell,Montana. Bill Sweeny would then hand the ranch down to John Sweeny. John would the marry Viola Cantrell and they would run the ranch until it was handed down to his daughter Sandy. Sandy would then marry Ross Hansen. The Ross Hansen ranch was began in 1976 and is still in operation today.Pete and Travis Hansen now run and Operate. The Ranch has 4 major brands they used on the ranch.The ranch runs over 1100 cow and calf pairs. The operation is a Black Angus operation.
