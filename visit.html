Come see the process and the way the operation is ran. The lower place is located in Dell, Montana. Then 20 miles up Big Sheep Creek Road you will see the upper place. The ranch sits 35 miles south of Dillon ,Montana. Dell, Montana, is a small town located in the southwestern part of the state, nestled in Beaverhead County. Known for its rural charm, Dell is surrounded by vast, open landscapes and scenic beauty, making it a quiet and peaceful place to live or visit. The town sits along the old U.S. Route 91, which once served as a major route for travelers heading through the region.
